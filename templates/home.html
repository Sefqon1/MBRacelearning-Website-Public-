About

I provide private lessons aimed to develop your speaking, listening, reading and writing skills. I teach/ tutor level A2-C2. My teaching style is relaxed and casual. I like to create an environment where my students feel comfortable speaking and expressing their ideas. I create unique and personalized lessons that best fit your learning needs.
I offer Business English, General English tutoring and Editing services.

About Me

Hi, I'm Morgan! I am originally from the United States. I recently moved from Germany to Vietnam. I have always had a passion for travelling and learning new languages. At 26, I quit my corporate job and decided to follow my dreams and become an English teacher and start my own teaching business.

If you want to follow my journey in Vietnam and learn more about me you can visit my blog!

Click here!

Experience:

I am a CELTA certified English teacher and tutor. I currently teach very young learners, teenagers and IELTS exam preparation at a language center in Hanoi. I have experience studying many languages. I have learned Spanish, German, Russian, French in school and self-taught.
The majority of my working experience is in business management, hospitality and customer service. It has taught me to be culturally sensitive, passionate, patient and adaptable.

Education:
I received my CELTA from Cambridge University, English Assessment in October 2020. In 2016, I graduated with my Bachelor’s degree in International Studies with a minor in linguistic anthropology from Virginia Commonwealth University.
